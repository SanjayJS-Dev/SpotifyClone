Questions?

We've got answers.

  • What is Spotify Premium?

    Spotify Premium is our paid membership service.
    Spotify Premium is available in Basic, Individual and Duo plans in South Korea.

  • Can I try Spotify Premium for free?

    Yes, if you haven’t tried Premium before, you can get 7 days of Spotify Premium free on 1 mobile phone device, with no payment details required. After that 7-day period, you can get your three months of Premium free when you subscribe to Basic or Individual plan. One month of Premium free is available when you subscribe to Duo plan.

  • How do I sign up for 7 days of Spotify Premium for free?

    You can sign up and try Spotify Premium free for 7 days only through the Spotify app on your mobile phone. At this time this offer is not available on any other devices.

  • Can I cancel Spotify Premium before my offer period ends?

    Yes, you can cancel anytime by accessing your accounts page.

  • How do I find a song on Spotify?

    Spotify has an easy to use music search feature that allows you to search a song of your choice. The iOS and Android apps also support lyrics search.
